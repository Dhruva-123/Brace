Section 06

Trust Score & Grade Verification Disclaimer

6.1 Trust Score Limitations

The Seller Credit Score generated by Brace is an algorithmic assessment based on platform-recorded data including transaction history, self-reported metrics, and feedback. It does NOT constitute:

  • A credit rating from a licensed credit rating agency
  • A guarantee of future performance or reliability
  • A background check, financial due diligence, or legal verification of any merchant
  • An endorsement or certification by any regulatory authority
⚠ Trust scores can be gamed, manipulated, or become outdated. They are supplementary indicators only. You must conduct independent due diligence on all counterparties before entering any trade transaction.

6.2 Grade Verification Limitations

Grades declared on Brace are self-reported by sellers. Conditional verification is triggered algorithmically but does not substitute for physical inspection by accredited inspection bodies such as SGS, Bureau Veritas, or Intertek. The Normalized Global Grade Score is a proprietary conversion system and is not recognized by any official grading authority.

6.3 Buyer Protection

Brace strongly recommends that buyers for high-value transactions engage accredited inspection services and independent quality verification regardless of a seller's trust score or declared grade.
